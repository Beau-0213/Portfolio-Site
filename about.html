Education

I have attended several schools at the high school level and one at the college level. In high school I spent my first two years at my local high school, taking foreign language, honors and AP classes. My last two years of high school were spent at the Ashtabula County Technical & Career center (A-Tech), which is a trade school at the high school level. At A-Tech, I was a student in the Visual Design & Imaging laboratory. This lab is focused on digital art and photography and touched on screen printing, embroidery, book binding, and animation. while at A-Tech, I earned numerous certifications, which are listed in the certification section below. I graduated high school with a Career Technical Honors Diploma in the top 30% of my class.


I started attending Kent State University in my junior year of high school as a College Credit Plus (CCP) student. CCP is a dual-enrollment program that allows high school students to take college classes at a college level with college students and college professors. The CCP program allowed me to take free college classes while in high school and have them count as high school and college credit. After graduating high school, I was accepted into Kent State as a full-time student. I declared an Associate of applied business in Business Management degree with a focus in Entrepreneurship and finished that degree in May of 2025. After graduating I declared a second degree, an Associate of applied business in Information Technology degree. I will graduate from this degree in May of 2026.
